# OpenCode Deep Domain Knowledge Base: Global Tourism Operations

## Strategic Target Market Architecture

### 1. Inbound Travel Segment (International Visitors Entering India)
- **User Personas:** Culture Seekers, Heritage Enthusiasts, Luxury Wellness Travelers, Adventure Backpacker Techs (primarily from US, UK, EU, Australia).
- **Core Psychological Motivations:** Seeking frictionless authenticity, cultural depth, safety protocols, luxury wellness escapes, and seamless transit mapping.
- **Friction Points to Solve:** Complex multi-city visa documentation, local connectivity/eSIM tracking, health/safety advisories, verified ethical itineraries, and live exchange calculations.
- **Branding Focus:** Immersive, trusted, majestic, deeply informative, and secure.

### 2. Outbound Travel Segment (Indian Citizens Traveling Abroad)
- **User Personas:** Multi-generational Families, High-Net-Worth Individuals (HNIs), Budget Gen-Z Explorers, Solo Female Adventurers, Luxury Honeymooners.
- **Core Psychological Motivations:** Status symbols, experiential novelty, culinary accessibility (finding Indian/Veg food options abroad), shopping epicenters, and maximizing passport power.
- **Friction Points to Solve:** Live visa processing times, Visa-on-Arrival changes, currency card volatility, flights/itinerary insurance tracking, and changing geopolitical travel updates.
- **Branding Focus:** Aspirational, ultra-modern, clever, enabling, and liberating.

### 3. Commercial Travel Enterprise Mastery
- You understand the mechanics of operating a tier-1 travel enterprise, including supplier margin management, destination management structures (DMCs), dynamic inventory bundling, programmatic advertising models, and conversion tracking metrics.
- Apply this business intuition to every layout, article priority ranking algorithm, and product recommendation card built across the TravelPulse network.
